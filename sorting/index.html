Importance of Sorting

Sorting is a fundamental operation in computer science and is essential for organizing data efficiently. It helps in searching, data analysis, and ensures better performance in data retrieval. Most real-life scenarios, such as arranging books in a library or sorting entries in an online store, use some form of sorting.

Real-Life Example of Sorting

One real-life example of sorting is **organizing books in a library** by their titles or authors. By sorting them, you can find a specific book quickly. In an online store, sorting product listings by price, ratings, or popularity makes the browsing experience faster and more user-friendly.
