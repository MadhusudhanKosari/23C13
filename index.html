History and Importance of DSA

Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) originated with the earliest developments in computer science, and have been instrumental in solving complex problems and optimizing systems over the decades. DSA is vital in making programs more scalable, faster, and efficient, enabling systems to handle large datasets and complex operations effectively.

Concepts Explained Point-Wise:

  • Primitive Data Types: Basic types such as integers, floats, characters.
  • Non-Primitive Data Types: Derived types like arrays, linked lists, trees.
  • Linear Data Structures: Data arranged sequentially, such as arrays and queues.
  • Non-Linear Data Structures: Hierarchical data arrangement like trees and graphs.
